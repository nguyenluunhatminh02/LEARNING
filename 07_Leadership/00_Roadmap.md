# 🎯 07 — Leadership & Engineering Management Curriculum

## Mục tiêu tổng quan
Phát triển kỹ năng lãnh đạo từ Tech Lead → Engineering Manager → Director → VP Engineering → CTO.

---

## Cấu trúc: 12 bài học — 4 phần

### Phần 1: Technical Leadership (Bài 01–03)
| Bài | Chủ đề | Nội dung chính |
|-----|--------|----------------|
| 01 | Tech Lead Fundamentals | Role definition, influence without authority, technical mentoring |
| 02 | Technical Communication | Writing RFCs, presenting to executives, stakeholder management |
| 03 | Cross-Team Collaboration | Dependency management, alignment, conflict resolution |

### Phần 2: People Management (Bài 04–06)
| Bài | Chủ đề | Nội dung chính |
|-----|--------|----------------|
| 04 | Hiring & Team Building | Interview design, hiring bar, diversity, onboarding |
| 05 | 1:1s, Feedback & Coaching | Radical candor, performance reviews, career development |
| 06 | Team Health & Culture | Psychological safety, team dynamics, remote leadership |

### Phần 3: Engineering Operations (Bài 07–09)
| Bài | Chủ đề | Nội dung chính |
|-----|--------|----------------|
| 07 | Project & Program Management | Estimation, planning, velocity, risk management |
| 08 | Engineering Processes | Sprint management, incident response, on-call, postmortems |
| 09 | Engineering Metrics & Productivity | DORA, SPACE, developer experience, tool investment |

### Phần 4: Executive Leadership (Bài 10–12)
| Bài | Chủ đề | Nội dung chính |
|-----|--------|----------------|
| 10 | Product Thinking for Engineers | Product-market fit, metrics, AB testing, user research |
| 11 | Business & Strategy | P&L, fundraising, budgeting, build vs buy, vendor management |
| 12 | CTO & VP Engineering Skills | Org design, technical strategy, board communication, scaling orgs |

---

## Lộ trình học

```
Tháng 1-2: Technical Leadership (Bài 01-03)
  → Applicable at Senior / Staff engineer level

Tháng 3-4: People Management (Bài 04-06)
  → For transitioning to Engineering Manager

Tháng 5-6: Engineering Operations (Bài 07-09)
  → For Senior EM / Director level

Tháng 7-8: Executive Leadership (Bài 10-12)
  → For Director → VP → CTO trajectory
```
