# 📚 Data Structures & Algorithms — Roadmap

## Tổng quan
Nền tảng tư duy giải quyết vấn đề — bắt buộc cho Staff Engineer trở lên.
**12 bài học** chia thành **4 phần**.

---

## 📁 01_Foundations (Bài 01–03)
| Bài | Tiêu đề | Nội dung chính |
|-----|---------|----------------|
| 01 | Complexity & Arrays | Big-O, arrays, strings, two pointers, sliding window |
| 02 | Linked Lists & Stacks/Queues | Singly/Doubly LL, Stack, Queue, Monotonic Stack |
| 03 | Hash Tables & Sets | Hash function, collision, HashMap patterns |

## 📁 02_Trees_Graphs (Bài 04–06)
| Bài | Tiêu đề | Nội dung chính |
|-----|---------|----------------|
| 04 | Trees & BST | Binary Tree, BST, DFS, BFS, balanced trees |
| 05 | Heaps & Priority Queues | Min/Max heap, top-K, median stream |
| 06 | Graphs | BFS, DFS, Dijkstra, topological sort, union-find |

## 📁 03_Advanced_Algorithms (Bài 07–09)
| Bài | Tiêu đề | Nội dung chính |
|-----|---------|----------------|
| 07 | Sorting & Searching | QuickSort, MergeSort, Binary Search variants |
| 08 | Dynamic Programming | Memoization, tabulation, classic DP patterns |
| 09 | Greedy & Backtracking | Greedy choice, backtracking template, pruning |

## 📁 04_System_Algorithms (Bài 10–12)
| Bài | Tiêu đề | Nội dung chính |
|-----|---------|----------------|
| 10 | Trie & Advanced Strings | Trie, KMP, Rabin-Karp, suffix array |
| 11 | Concurrency & Parallelism | Threading, locks, concurrent data structures |
| 12 | System-Level Algorithms | Consistent hashing, Bloom filter, HyperLogLog, skip list |
